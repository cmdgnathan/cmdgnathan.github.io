Nathaniel Moore Glaser

Nathaniel Moore Glaser

I am a Software Engineer at Zoox (Amazon’s robotaxi subsidiary), where I lead efforts in collision avoidance, especially around quantifying our robot’s reaction to exceptionally rare events. My work spans ML, computer vision, large-scale data, and agentic workflows.

I received my Ph.D. in Robotics from Georgia Tech, where I had the privilege of working with Prof. Zsolt Kira. My research helped found the area of collaborative perception — multi-agent visual systems that learn what, when, and how to communicate.

Before Zoox, I deployed vision-based quality control across three commercial farms at Bowery Farming and gave sight to a strawberry-harvesting “octopus” robot at Traptic.
